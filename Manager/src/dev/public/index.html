Welcome to Luther

      
Your all-in-one companion for Realm of the Mad God. Let's take a quick tour so you know where everything is.

      
This will only take a minute.

    


    
    

      
1 / 4

      
Home

      
The Home tab is your command center. From here you can:

      
    
        
  • Active Script — Quick link to the Scripts tab for disk-based .js scripts.
    • 
        
  • Session Stats — Track uptime, fame gained, white bags, events killed, and dungeons completed in real time.
    • 
        
  • Accounts Ready — Quickly launch any of your saved accounts directly from the dashboard.
    • 
        
  • Edit Layout — Click the pencil icon in the top corner to rearrange, hide, or restore cards. Drag to reorder and save your custom layout.
    • 
      

    


    
    

      
2 / 4

      
Accounts

      
The Accounts tab is where you prepare sessions and automation. From here you can:

      
    
        
  • Manage accounts — Store credentials, server preferences, and proxy settings.
    • 
        
  • Scripts — Use disk-based .js scripts for automation when Developer Mode is on.
    • 
        
  • Accounts — Add email accounts to launch and manage them.
    • 
      

      
Start with accounts, then layer scripts once you are comfortable.

    


    
    

      
3 / 4

      
Accounts

      
This is where you manage your Realm accounts. You can store multiple accounts, view character overviews, vault contents, and launch into the game.

      
Let's add your first account now to get you started.

4 / 4

      
Damage Sniffer

      
The Damage Sniffer records combat data while you play. It tracks:

      
    
        
  • Runs — Each encounter is logged with a live view and saved history.
    • 
        
  • Targets — See every enemy you hit, filter by boss or miniboss.
    • 
        
  • Player Breakdown — View per-player DPS and damage contributions.
    • 
      

      
Data is captured automatically — just play and review your stats anytime.
